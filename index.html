Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our process and services.

The timeline varies depending on the destination country and service required. Typically, documentation and attestation take 2–4 weeks, while visa processing can range from 4 to 12 weeks. During your free consultation, we provide a personalised estimated timeline based on your specific case.

Requirements differ by destination and visa type. Generally, you will need educational certificates, experience letters, passport copies, photographs, and professional qualifications. Our consultants will provide you with a comprehensive, country-specific document checklist during your initial consultation.

We currently support placements and visa guidance for 25+ countries including Canada, Germany, the United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Ireland, and Singapore. Contact us to enquire about your specific destination.

Yes, placement assistance is one of our core services. We have strong relationships with reputable employers across 25+ countries and actively work to match your qualifications and experience with suitable overseas job opportunities in your field.
